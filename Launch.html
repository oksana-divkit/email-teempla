Some of the key capabilities introduced with the
New Version 3.0 of Teempla are:
 
 
Projects Dashboard
 
Provides a comprehensive overview of all your active projects and tasks within it. It highlights 3 Critical Actions and 3 Critical Tasks assigned to you and overall status of the project. Clicking on a Project Tile reveals all Actions, Tasks, Team Members, and Conversations concerning the project.
 
More on Quick Tutorial
 
 
 
Dynamic Viewer
 
A high-performance Viewer to quickly download large and complex Design Files on mobile TouchPads and Laptops. Teempla’s Dynamic Viewer contains powerful tools for real-time collaboration – Freehand Drawing, Sketching, and Type-
writing. It also includes tools for adding Sticky Notes, Pictures, and Documents at any point on the Floor-plan.

Live News Feed
 
Running News Feed from all project team members as they suggest changes, modify design files, and complete tasks. Forge trustworthy teams with Live Interactive Communications and instantaneously learn about project bottlenecks
and status.

Instantaneous Approvals
 
Quickly review and approve design changes and workarounds for timely execution of projects. You may also request review and approval of design changes suggested by you from specific stakeholders.
